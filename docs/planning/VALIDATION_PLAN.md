# Smalltalk Coach — Validation Plan: Wedge Interviews + Willingness-to-Pay Probe

Purpose: first validation step for the segment lock (`DECISIONS.md` → "2026-07-17 — V1 Target Segment: Relocation Wedge"). Documentation-only phase; no product is built until the thresholds below are evaluated.

## Target Sample

- 10–15 interviews.
- Screener: relocated to a new city or country within the last 6 months for work or school.
- Mix: roughly half students (including international students), half early-career professionals.
- Prefer people with in-person office/campus environments (the forced-daily-interaction trait); fully remote relocators are secondary.

## Recruitment Channels

- University international-student offices and conversation-partner programs.
- City and expat subreddits (r/expats, city subs), newcomer groups.
- New-grad and intern communities and Discords; LinkedIn outreach to recent movers.

## Interview Method (Mom Test Style)

- Recent specific situations, not opinions: "Tell me about the most recent time you didn't know what to say at work / on campus. What happened?"
- Frequency: how many moments like that in the past two weeks?
- Current alternatives: what did they do afterward (Google, ChatGPT, a friend, nothing)? Why wasn't it enough?
- Emotional and practical cost: skipped events, avoided people, missed connections.
- Do not pitch the product before the willingness-to-pay probe.

## Willingness-to-Pay Probe

- After the pain questions, describe the concept in one sentence.
- Pricing ladder: at what monthly price is this a clear yes / think twice / clearly not worth it? (Market anchor: Gleam at $12.99/mo or $69.99/yr.)
- Concrete commitment ask: waitlist signup with the price shown, or a refundable preorder. Record actual signups, not verbal enthusiasm.

## Decision Thresholds

- **Lock holds:** ≥8 of 15 report the pain at least weekly AND ≥5 of 15 give a concrete willingness-to-pay signal (signup or preorder at a stated price) → proceed to v1 lesson-path design.
- **Below thresholds:** trigger the revisit per the decision entry (re-examine segment or payer mix).
- Also record the students-vs-professionals split on pain frequency and willingness to pay — this decides the primary payer.

## Outputs

- Evidence Log rows in `DECISIONS.md` per interview batch.
- A follow-up decision entry moving the segment from Experiment toward Confirmed or Revisit.
